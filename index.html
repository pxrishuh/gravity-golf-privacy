Summary

Information the Game Stores

Gravity Golf may store limited information locally on your device for gameplay purposes, such as:

This information is stored using device-local storage (for example, via SharedPreferences) and generally stays on your device unless you uninstall the Game or clear the app data.

Information We Do Not Collect (by Default)

By default, the Game is designed to work without collecting:
